2018-2024 Honda Civic Key Fob Programming Instructions

Before You Start

You must have at least one working key fob to program additional remotes. If you have no working keys, you'll need a locksmith with Honda diagnostic equipment.

What You'll Need

  • One working programmed key fob ‚Äî your existing remote
  • New key fob(s) to program ‚Äî with fresh battery installed
  • 5 uninterrupted minutes ‚Äî timing is important

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. 1
    Get inside the vehicle with all key fobs

    Gather all the key fobs you want to program, including your existing working fob. Close all doors.

  2. 2
    Insert your working key into the ignition

    Turn the key to the ON position (don't start the engine). The dashboard lights should come on.

  3. 3
    Press the LOCK button on your working key fob

    Within 5 seconds of turning the ignition ON, press the LOCK button on your currently programmed remote.

  4. 4
    Turn the ignition OFF and remove the key

    Do this within 5 seconds of pressing LOCK. The timing matters!

  5. 5
    Repeat steps 2-4 three more times

    You'll do this sequence a total of 4 times. On the 4th cycle, leave the ignition in the ON position.

    üí° Pro Tip: Count out loud to keep track. "Cycle 1... Cycle 2... Cycle 3... Cycle 4 ‚Äî stay ON!"
  6. 6
    Listen for the locks to cycle

    After the 4th turn to ON, the door locks will automatically lock and unlock. This confirms you're in programming mode.

  7. 7
    Press LOCK on each new remote

    Within 10 seconds, press the LOCK button on each new key fob you want to program. The locks will cycle to confirm each one.

    ‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Any remotes not programmed during this session will be erased! Make sure to program ALL your remotes.
  8. 8
    Turn ignition OFF to exit programming mode

    Turn the key to OFF and remove it. Your key fobs are now programmed!

Troubleshooting

Locks didn't cycle after 4th turn
The timing was off. You need to complete each ON‚ÜíLOCK‚ÜíOFF cycle within 5 seconds. Start over and try to be quicker with the sequence.
New remote doesn't work after programming
  • Check the battery in the new remote ‚Äî it may be dead or installed incorrectly
  • Make sure you pressed LOCK within 10 seconds of entering programming mode
  • Verify your remote is compatible with your specific Civic trim/year
My Civic has push-button start
Push-button start Civics with smart keys cannot be self-programmed. You'll need to visit a Honda dealer or an automotive locksmith with Honda HDS diagnostic equipment. Expect to pay $50-100 for programming.
