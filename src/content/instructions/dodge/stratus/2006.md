Programming Instructions:

Note: This is the same for many Chrysler/Dodge/Jeep vehicles.

Grab your working remote.  Push the “Lock” button five times at one second intervals. Insert the key into the ignition and turn it to run. With the remote, push and hold the “Lock” button for 10 seconds. The remote is now programmed to the vehicle.
