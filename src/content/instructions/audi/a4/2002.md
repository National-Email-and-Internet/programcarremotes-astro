Programming Instructions:

Audi A4 key fob programming instructions are available for several model years.

Programming Instructions:

You need TWO regular keys (not valet key) for this system to work

1.  Open the driver door , keep it open, and sit in the drivers seat (make sure you have both keys with you)
2.  Put the key #1 in the ignition and turn it to the on position BUT without starting the car
3.  Close the drivers door
4.  Lock the drivers door with key #2
5.  Push both buttons on key #2 and hold them for at least 10 seconds or until the lights flash and the horn beeps.
6.  The key has been succesfully programmed

8.  Take the key out of the ignition
9.  Close the door
10.  Lock the door with key #2
