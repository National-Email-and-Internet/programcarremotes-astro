**Programming Instructions:**

Take the key out of the ignition and close all of the doors (while sitting inside). Open the driver’s side door. Insert the key. Turn it to “On.” Turn it to “off.” Remove it. Close then open the driver’s door three times within 10 seconds. Insert the key and turn it to “On.” Remove it. Push “Lock” on the remote. The remote is programmed.
