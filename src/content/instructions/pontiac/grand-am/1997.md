Learn to **program the remote** to your **1997 Pontiac Grand Am** without any help from the dealer. This includes the **GT** model.

Programming Instructions: Note: This is the same for many GM vehicles.

Open the trunk. Look in the left side of the trunk. You’ll find a white connector with two holes. Insert the ends of the jumper pin wire into each hole. This jumps the programming connector. The door’s will lock then unlock. Push and hold “Lock” on the remote you are programming for five seconds. This programs the remote to the vehicle. Close the trunk.
