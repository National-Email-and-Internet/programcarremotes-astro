Learn to **program the remote** to your **2007 Pontiac Grand Prix w**ithout any help from the dealer. This includes the **Grand Prix GT** model.

Programming Instructions: Note: This is the same for many GM vehicles.

Sit in the driver’s seat. Take the key out. Open the interior fuse box panel. Inspect the fuses to find “Mall Pgm.” Pull the fuse out. Close the door and sit inside. Turn the ignition key to “Acc.” Turn it off. Turn it back to “Acc.” Open the driver’s door. Close the driver’s door. You’ll hear a beep. Push and hold “Lock” and “Unlock” together for 17 seconds to program the remote.
