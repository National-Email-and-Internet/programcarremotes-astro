Programming Instructions:

Sit in the driver’s seat. Close all of the doors with the exception of the driver’s door. Lock all of the doors with the exception of the driver’s door. Insert the key into the ignition but don’t turn it. Remove the key. Follow this sequence using the driver’s side door lock button: Lock, Unlock, Lock, Unlock, Lock, Unlock, Lock, Unlock, Lock, then Unlock. Close and then open the driver’s door.

Follow this sequence using the driver’s side door lock button: Lock, Unlock, Lock, Unlock, Lock, Unlock, Lock, Unlock, Lock, then Unlock.  Insert the key. Turn it to “On.” Turn it to “Off.”

Push and hold “Lock” on the remote. Close and then open the driver’s side door. The remote is now programmed.
