Programming Instructions:

Sit in the driver’s seat. Open the driver’s door. Grasp the multifunction beam switch (high beam headlights). Pull it toward you and hold it in the position. Insert the key. Turn the key to “Acc.” Release the high beam switch, and then pull it rearward four times.

Push the “Lock” button on the remote to program it.
