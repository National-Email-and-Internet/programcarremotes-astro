Learn to program the remote in your **2001 Volkswagen Jetta.** This is also called the **VW Jetta.**

**Programming Instructions:** 

Insert your key into the ignition and turn it to “On” without cranking. Open the driver’s side door. Put your spare key in the driver’s door slot and turn it to “Unlock.” Hold it in the position until you hear three beeps. This takes up to 20 seconds. Release the key but leave it in the door slot.

Push “Lock” or “Unlock” on the key remote fob. Turn the spare key in the door to the left, and then release it. Remove it, and then turn off the ignition key and remove it. Test the remote.
